## About IBA Music

IBA Music is operated by **Island Breeze Affiliates Inc.**, a corporation
organized under the laws of the State of Florida, USA. We book and manage
live-music performances at venues including theme parks, resorts, private
events, and concert venues.

The apps and web tools referenced on this site are built **for our own
operations** — they are used by the musicians, band leaders, office staff,
and authorized contractors who work with IBA Music. They are not
general-purpose consumer products.

If you believe you have reached this site in error, you can safely close
this tab. None of the tools referenced are available to the general public.

## Products covered

The Privacy Policy and Terms of Service on this site apply to:

- **IBA Companion** for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and watchOS
- **IBA Companion** for Android
- **IBA Music Admin Dashboard** at admin.ibamusic.com
- IBA Music's internal web tools (musician availability forms, invoice
  portal, band leader schedule tools)
