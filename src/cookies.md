## This subdomain (legal.ibamusic.com)

**No cookies.** This site sets no cookies of any kind. It loads no
third-party scripts, no analytics, no advertising code, and no
CDN-hosted fonts. You can verify this in your browser's developer
tools — the network tab will show only HTML, CSS, and the occasional
favicon served from this same origin.

The legal subdomain exists to publish transparency documents. It
would be inconsistent with that purpose to track the people reading
them.

## IBA Music mobile apps

**IBA Companion** for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, and Android does
not use HTTP cookies. Mobile apps authenticate via OAuth 2.0 tokens
or passkey assertions stored in the device keychain (iOS) or
keystore (Android) and sent with API requests as HTTP authorization
headers. No cookie is set or read by any IBA Music mobile app.

## IBA Music Admin Dashboard (admin.ibamusic.com)

The admin dashboard uses **strictly-necessary, functional cookies**
to keep IBA Music staff signed in. These cookies:

- Store an authenticated session identifier.
- Are set only after explicit sign-in via Microsoft Azure Active
  Directory, Google, Apple, or passkey.
- Are marked `HttpOnly`, `Secure`, and `SameSite=Lax`.
- Expire when you sign out, when your session idles out, or when
  your authorization is revoked by an administrator.
- Are **not** used for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, Segment, or any
other third-party tracking or analytics tool on the admin dashboard
or any other IBA Music web property.

## IBA Music satellite web tools

The availability forms at forms.ibamusic.com, the invoice portal at
invoice.ibamusic.com, and the band leader tools at
lead.ibamusic.com follow the same rule: functional session cookies
only for the sign-in that gates each tool, no tracking, no
advertising, no third-party analytics.

## Your choices

Because IBA Music properties use only strictly-necessary functional
cookies for sign-in, there is no "accept / reject" cookie banner to
show you. Under the ePrivacy Directive and similar rules, consent
is not required for cookies that are strictly necessary for a
service explicitly requested by the user. Signing in to the admin
dashboard is that explicit request.

If you configure your browser to block cookies from our admin or
web-tool domains, you will not be able to sign in to those tools.
The rest of your browsing, including reading this site, will work
as expected.

## Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page if
this policy changes. If any IBA Music product ever begins to use
tracking cookies or third-party analytics, we will update this page
before the change takes effect.
