This page lists the contact paths for **privacy, legal, and security
matters** relating to IBA Music products and services. It is **not**
a general support channel — if you are an IBA Music musician needing
app help, please use the in-app support options or contact your
band leader directly.

## Privacy, legal, and data-subject requests

**Email:**
[privacy@ibamusic.com](mailto:privacy@ibamusic.com)

This inbox handles:

- Access, correction, deletion, and export requests under the
  [Privacy Policy](/privacy) (sections 8, 9, and 10).
- General privacy questions.
- Questions about the [Terms of Service](/terms).
- Questions about the Google Calendar integration and the
  `calendar.app.created` OAuth scope.

We respond to privacy inquiries within the timeframes required by
GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws. For general questions, please allow
up to 5 business days.

When sending a request, please include:

- The account email or other identifier we use for your account.
- The nature of the request (access, correction, deletion, export,
  objection, opt-out).
- Any information that helps us verify your identity — we cannot
  release personal data to an unverified requester.

## Security vulnerabilities and responsible disclosure

Also **[privacy@ibamusic.com](mailto:privacy@ibamusic.com)**,
ideally with a subject line starting with "Security —".

See the [Security](/security) page for our full responsible
disclosure policy, including scope, acknowledgements, and the
commitments we make to researchers who report in good faith.

## Business, press, and partnerships

This legal subdomain exists to publish transparency documents and
support Google OAuth verification. It is not a public-relations
channel. If you are a journalist, a potential business partner, or
a platform representative, please reach out through IBA Music's
public-facing channels or contact our staff directly if you already
know them.
